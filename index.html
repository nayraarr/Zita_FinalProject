About Us

Ecisco was founded in 2022 with the aim of creating a large, easily-accessible library of free-to-read quality stories, poems, fairy tales and comics for children. Ecisco offers both classic and contemporary stories in an easy-to-read format with vibrant illustrations designed to excite children and engage them around reading.
